# wifi2cot
 ATAK Plugin that grab RSSI values from local Access Points and try to plot where they are on the map.

# Directions
 The operations are simple, press Start to begin scanning for WIFI Access Points (AP) and nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) emitters. As soon as results are collected they appear in the in-app list—tap any entry to begin or stop live tracking of that specific signal source. The longer and the more you move around the area of interest the better. When ready, press Stop and then Compute. This will take all of the collected signal measurements and try to triangluate where the emitters are.

 Note: You will likely want to disable WIFI scan throttling in the Android Developer settings menu otherwise your scans will not be as frequent as possible.
